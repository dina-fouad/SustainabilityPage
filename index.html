🌱 Our Sustainability Commitment

At Jordan Classical Tours, our mission is to provide high-quality, authentic travel experiences while preserving the natural environment, supporting local communities, and respecting Jordan’s rich cultural heritage.

We are committed to responsible tourism practices that create positive impacts for our guests, partners, and the destinations we serve, ensuring that future generations can continue to experience the beauty of Jordan.

📜 Our Sustainability Policy

  • Sustainability is part of our daily operations and long-term strategy at Jordan Classical Tours.
  • We are committed to minimizing environmental, social, and cultural impacts while creating positive contributions to local communities and destinations.
  • Our approach covers all aspects of our business, including suppliers, transport, accommodations, excursions, and customer communication.
  • We promote environmental protection through responsible use of energy, water, and resources.
  • We support local communities and respect cultural heritage.
  • We are committed to human rights, child protection, animal welfare, and transparent communication with our clients and partners.
  • Our sustainability performance is continuously monitored and improved through regular reviews, supplier assessments, and customer feedback.

⚙️ Sustainability Practices

By integrating the following sustainable practices into our operations, we aim to provide transformative travel experiences that not only captivate our guests but also contribute to a more sustainable and responsible future for Jordan and the global tourism industry:

🌱 Internal Management

Minimizing environmental impact through efficient resource use, energy-saving practices, and staff engagement in sustainability initiatives.

🌍 Environmental Initiatives

Reducing carbon footprint through waste reduction, recycling, water conservation, and responsible resource management.

🏘️ Community Engagement

Supporting local businesses, artisans, and community-based tourism to contribute to local economic development.

🌐 Responsible Tourism

Promoting cultural respect, protecting heritage, and encouraging responsible behavior among our guests.

🏨 Sustainable Accommodations

Selecting accommodations that prioritize environmental and social responsibility, including locally owned and eco-friendly properties.

🚗 Sustainable Transport

Optimizing routes, encouraging shared transport, and working with responsible transport providers to reduce emissions.

🎓 Traveler Education

Providing pre-trip and on-tour information to encourage responsible travel practices and cultural awareness.

🗺️ Excursions and Activities

Designing low-impact experiences that respect nature, culture, and local communities.

👥 Guides & Local Representatives

Training guides to promote sustainability, cultural understanding, and responsible tourism practices.

📍 Destination Responsibility

Supporting sustainable destination management through collaboration with local stakeholders and initiatives.

🛡️ Customer Communication & Protection

Ensuring transparent communication, accurate information, and the safety and well-being of our guests.

These practices guide how we plan, operate, and continuously improve our tours.

🌍 Responsible Travel Tips

We invite you to travel responsibly while exploring Jordan. By following simple guidelines, you can help protect the environment, support local communities, and preserve the country’s rich cultural heritage.

🎭 Cultural Respect

  • Respect local culture, traditions, and dress codes
  • Travel with an open mind and respect for local ways of life

🏘️ Community Support

  • Support local businesses, artisans, and communities

♻️ Environmental Responsibility

  • Reduce waste by using reusable bottles and bags
  • Save water and energy, especially in water-scarce areas

📸 Respect & Privacy

  • Ask permission before taking photos of people

🐾 Ethical Choices

  • Avoid buying products that harm wildlife or heritage

🧭 Safety & Guidance

  • Follow your guide’s instructions and safety guidelines
